Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Listmate ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Listmate mobile application and website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Google Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your name and email address from Google. We use this solely to identify you and associate your account with your household.

Household Data: Grocery lists, store names, items, and visit history that you create within the app. This data is visible only to members of your household.

Usage Data: We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising services of any kind.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the grocery list service
  • To associate you with your household's shared lists
  • To send household invitation emails (via SendGrid, with tracking disabled)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers (Render, PostgreSQL) with access restricted to the application layer. Authentication is handled entirely through Google Sign-In — we never see or store your password.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us
  • You can leave a household to revoke data visibility from other members
  • You can request a copy of your data

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: hello@grocerlist.app

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do so, send an email to venragh@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will delete your data within 7 business days. You may also delete your data directly from the app by visiting Settings → Delete Account.
